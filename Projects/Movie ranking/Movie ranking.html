The Best Marvel According to Me

My 3 movies of all-time

The Avengers (2012)

Positive: Successfully brought multiple heroes together in one cohesive, thrilling team-up.

Negative: Predictable plot and a somewhat underwhelming villain in Loki's invasion plan.

Avengers: Infinity War (2018)

Positive: Bold storytelling with a shocking, emotional cliffhanger and a compelling villain in Thanos.

Negative: Fast-paced plot with limited screen time for some characters and subplots.

Avengers: Endgame (2019)

Positive: Epic conclusion with emotional payoffs, nostalgic callbacks, and a satisfying character arc closure.

Negative: Time travel logic is inconsistent, and some fans felt key characters got rushed endings.
